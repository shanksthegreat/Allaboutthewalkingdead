About The Walking Dead

The Walking Dead is a popular post-apocalyptic horror television series based on the comic book series of the Walking dead

The series is set in a world overrun by zombies, also known as "walkers." It follows a group of survivors led by characters such as Rick Grimes as they navigate the challenges of living in a world where the dead have risen. The survivors face not only external threats from the walkers but also internal conflicts among themselves and encounters with other groups of survivors.
